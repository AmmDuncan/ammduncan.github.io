I design and build beautiful, strategic landing pages for businesses. A landing page is where you give well thought out information about your products and services to guide visitors to take a desired action; purchase a product, sign up for a service, or call you.

I design and build web applications that run on the content you feed it. They allow you to handle the stored data to derive value in efficiency, accuracy, and effectiveness. Such apps include blogs, online stores, and customer management systems.
