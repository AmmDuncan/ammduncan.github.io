What I do...

Landing Page Design and Development

I design and build beautiful, goal-directed landing pages for businesses. A landing page is where you as a business owner, provide the necessary information about your business or product to encourage the visitor to take an action; purchase a product, sign up for a service, or give you a call.

Dynamic Websites Design and Development

I design and build websites (web applications) whose content vary depending on what data you have collected and stored. These applications allow you to manipulate the data you've stored to allow you derive value in the form of efficiency, accuracy and effectiveness.

Why Choose Me?

Rapid Prototyping

Your application is prototyped as soon as possible, hence, you get to see and approve how your application should flow.

Collaborative Development

You get consistent updates with your project's progress. Therefore, you get to be a part of the development process.

Goal-Directed Development

Each feature of your app is designed to satisfy an objective you specify. As such, you don’t have to deal with unnecessary and distracting features.

My Work

I’ve worked closely with various institutions including schools, churches and businesses and helped each of them achieve their objectives; I designed and built static websites to give necessary information to visitors of their websites, as well as, web applications to store and manage data.
